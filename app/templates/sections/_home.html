Tran Chau

Hi, I’m Tran Chau — a model with a love for simplicity, softness, and stories told through little details. My style is playful and minimalistic, often inspired by the gentle beauty of bunnies, deer, and quiet moments. I believe in creating images that feel alive yet calm, where warmth and curiosity meet. Sometimes I sign my work as MuranoCraft, a name that reflects my love for delicate craftsmanship and subtle artistry. Outside of modeling, I’m learning Korean, discovering the rhythm of new words, and finding joy in how languages can carry feeling. I also adore K-pop, long walks, and baggy clothes that make me feel both small and free. And if you know the inside joke — yes, I’m the pearl, and he’s the oyster.
